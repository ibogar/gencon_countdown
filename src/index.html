Board games

Step into a world of strategy, storytelling, and fun! Whether you're into quick party games or epic campaigns, GenCon is the perfect place to discover new favorites and meet fellow board game enthusiasts. Pull up a chair and let the games begin!

Card games

Calling all deck-builders and card-slingers! From casual play to high-stakes tournaments, GenCon offers a vibrant space for trading card game fans to play, trade, and test their skills. Try new sets, meet other players, and sharpen your strategy!

And a lot more!

Beyond the tables, GenCon is packed with exciting things to do! Explore artist alleys, join creative workshops, meet special guests, and dive into cosplay or LARPing adventures. There's something for everyone—come experience the full magic of the con!
